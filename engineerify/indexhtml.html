WHAT ARE YOU PREPARING FOR?

1

JEE

Conducted by NTA, this is the gateway to NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions.

2

JEE-ADVANCE

For admission into the IITs, you must first qualify JEE Main and then clear JEE Advanced.

3

MHT-CET

For admission to engineering colleges in Maharashtra.

4

MET

Conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) for admission to its engineering courses.

5

BITSAT

Conducted by BITS Pilani for admission to its campuses in Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad.

6

VITEEE

Entrance exam for admission to VIT University campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, and Amaravati
